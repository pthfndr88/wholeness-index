BODY
The Wholeness Index is built on the Trapezium Model — a

professional must develop to move from capability to impact.

FOUR DOMAIN CARDS (display as horizontal cards or stacked list):

  IDENTITY — Know
  Clarity about who you are, what drives you,
  and what you're specifically here to contribute.

  CHARACTER — Be Strong
  The inner qualities that hold under pressure:
  consistency, courage, discipline, integrity.

  COMPETENCE — Be Capable
  Developed skills, reliable execution,
  and the capacity to solve novel problems.

  IMPACT — Do
  The tangible difference you create —
  recognised, sustainable, and aligned with your purpose.

When these four are aligned, knowledge flows into strength,
and strength flows into action.

When even one is underdeveloped, you feel the friction —
the gap between your potential and your reality.

The Wholeness Index locates the gap precisely.

BODY (above the archetype cards)
The Trapezium Model generates a structural profile —
a precise picture of how your four dimensions relate to
each other and where the constraint lies.

FIVE ARCHETYPE CARDS (display with mini Trapezium SVG for each):

  THE SEEKER
  "Knowledge without strength"
  You know yourself with uncommon depth. The gap is
  translating that self-knowledge into durable capability
  the world can feel.
  Core issue: Identity runs ahead of character.

  THE POWERHOUSE
  "Strength without direction"
  Enormous capacity. Consistent execution. But without a
  settled sense of what it's all for, the power scatters.
  Core issue: Strength without self-knowledge.

  THE HIDDEN GEM
  "Potential without visibility"
  Alignment across identity, character, and competence —
  but the work isn't reaching the people who should see it.
  Core issue: Full potential, unrecognised.

  THE FRAGMENTED
  "Scattered energy"
  Pockets of strength across all four dimensions, but no
  domain is fully developed. Multiple competing needs.
  Core issue: Energy diffused rather than directed.

  THE ALIGNED LEADER
  "Compound formation"
  All four dimensions reinforce each other. The question
  is no longer how to break through — it's how to multiply.
  Core issue: Sustaining and scaling what's already working.

BODY (below cards)
Which one are you?

The free assessment classifies your pattern in about
8 minutes — using 15 carefully constructed statements
rated on how consistently they describe you as you
actually are, not as you aspire to be.

COPY NOTES FOR IMPLEMENTATION
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

TIMING
All references say "about 8 minutes" — not 3.
This is accurate and signals depth rather than triviality.

TONE
The voice throughout is editorial and precise, not conversational
or hype-driven. Avoid: "game-changing", "unlock your potential",
"transform your life", "just", "simply", "easy".

ARCHETYPE NAMES
Always capitalised as proper nouns:
The Seeker · The Powerhouse · The Hidden Gem ·
The Fragmented · The Aligned Leader

FRAMEWORK NAME
"Trapezium Model" with trademark ® on first use per page.
"Wholeness Index" with trademark ™ on first use per page.

SECTION MARKERS
No emojis. Section headers only. The design system
(JetBrains Mono eyebrows) carries the visual hierarchy.

WHAT TO A/B TEST FIRST:
1. Hero subheadline — this version vs "You've done everything
   right. That's what makes it so confusing."
2. Pattern section position — before or after the framework
   explanation (hypothesis: archetypes first converts better)
3. CTA button text — "Take the free assessment" vs
   "Find your pattern" vs "Begin the Index"
